She must have needed less help feeling safe by the time she changed her name from Stef to Crystal. And she must have wanted something, too, something that warranted a little less safety and a little more risk.

It was those two , of course. They were the reason for the feeling of safety and the reason for the wanting. Her new name was a gift when she heard it from them. It sounded to her like they were saying, "Precious." "Precious."
